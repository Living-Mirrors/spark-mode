# Security Policy

## Scope

This repository contains cognitive specifications — text documents, not executable code. There is no runtime, no API, no user data processing.

## Reporting

If you discover a concern with the specifications (e.g., a principle that could produce harmful AI behavior, a research citation that is incorrect or misleading), please report it to:

**Email:** attila@livingmirrors.ai

We take the accuracy and safety of these specifications seriously. All reports will be reviewed within 7 days.

## Responsible Use

These specifications are designed to enhance AI cognition in constructive ways. They are released under the MIT License with no restrictions on use. However, we encourage users to apply them thoughtfully and to consider the implications of changing how an AI perceives, relates, generates, or learns.
